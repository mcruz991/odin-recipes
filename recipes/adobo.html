Adobo

Description

This is a dish originatign from the philippines containing pork. It could also be made with fish or chicken. The original ingredients contain pig ears but any part of the pork will do. It has a very crispy taste to it and a very tangy notes with calamansi and onions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pig ears
  • 1 1/2 lb pork belly
  • 1 piece of onion minced
  • 3 tablespoons soy suace
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 knob ginger minced
  • 3 tablespoons chili flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 lemon or 3-5 pc of calamansi
  • 1/2 butter
